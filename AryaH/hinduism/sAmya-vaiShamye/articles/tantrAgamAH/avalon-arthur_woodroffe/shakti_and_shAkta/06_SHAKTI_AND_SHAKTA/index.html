Shakti who is in Herself pure blissful Consciousness (Cidrupini) is also the Mother of Nature and is Nature itself born of the creative play of Her thought. Further, it is a common error to confound two distinct things, namely, belief and practice and the written records of it. When I speak of the ancient past of this faith I am not referring merely to thewritings which exist today which are called Tantras. A vast number of Tantras have disappeared probably for ever. Of those which survive a large number are unknown. Most of those which are available are of fragmentary character.

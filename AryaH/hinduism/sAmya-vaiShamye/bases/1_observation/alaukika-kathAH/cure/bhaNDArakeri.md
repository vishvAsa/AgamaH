A friend who is a secretary to the current pIThAdhipati of bhaNDArakeri maTha was about to ask for leave so as to attend to his brother's soon-to-die infant. The mAThAdhipati told him to keep the infant in ICU for a few more hours while he did some japa all night. The child survived and grew up healthy.

вАЬThe cognitions derived from the Veda must be right,вАФbecause they arise from the eternal sentence,вАФlike the cognition of the sentence itself.вАФThe arguments detailed before are all applicable hereвАЭ.вАФ[≈ЪlokavƒБrtika-sambandhƒБkбє£epa, 2].вАФ(2347).

That which produces a cognition, independently of the person using it, with regard to a certain thing, cannot be false in respect of that thing; e.g. the Word as bringing about the cognition of its own meaning;вАФthe Vedic Sentence produces the cognition of its meaning, independently of any speaker in the shape of its Author;вАФhence there is apprehension of a condition contrary to the wider condition;вАФвАШfalsityвАЩ being вАШless wideвАЩ than вАШbeing productive of cognitions through dependence upon the composing PersonвАЩ, which latter thus is the вАШvyƒБpakaвАЩ, the вАШwider conditionвАЩ,вАФthe вАШviruddhaвАЩ, вАШcontraryвАЩ, of this would be вАШbeing productive of cognitions independently of the composing personвАЩ; and this latter condition is apprehended in the case of the Veda.

вАШLike the Word in the expression of its meaningвАЩ;вАФthis is meant to show that Words are productive of cognitions in conformity with the real state of things; and the вАШcognition produced by the wordвАЩ is not meant to be the Corroborative Instance; the words themselves being that instance.вАФThus then, in the argument, Word is the Minor Term; and what is sought to be proved in regard to it is its non-falsity on account of its being the cause of cognitions in conformity with the real state of things -consequently, the Corroborative Instance also should have exactly the same character; otherwise the Instance would be devoid of the Probandum.

There is another argument, which is formulated as follows:вАФThat Cognition which is brought about by an eternal sentence must be in conformity with the real state of things and hence right;вАФjust like the cognition of the verbal form of the sentence itself;вАФthe cognition of the fact of the performance of Agnihotra, etc. leading to Heaven is produced by the eternal sentence;вАФhence this is a Reason based upon the nature of things.вАФThe eternality (of the Veda) has been proved in detail already; hence the Probans in this argument cannot be said to be вАШInadmissibleвАЩ.

вАШThe Cognit ion brought about by the Vedic Injunction, etc. etc.вАЩвАФThis has been already explained.
